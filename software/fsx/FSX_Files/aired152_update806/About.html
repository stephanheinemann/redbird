About AirEd

AirEd was written by me as an easier way of delving into the mostly unknown area of FS98 AIR files. The only tool available previously was extremely limited and very inflexible. AirDec was available for FS95, but was never converted to FS98. Although somewhat flexible, it was very difficult for the average person to use.

Future Plans:

  1. I plan on making the text types editable in much the same way that numbers are. To do so, I will need to know what the limits are (if any?) on the lengths of the individual text records.
  2. The Hex editor code was not written by me. One of these days, I'll write a really good Hex Edit control. Until then, we'll all have to live with the quirks of the one that's there.
